# Job Fit Criteria — Igor Pivnyk

## Employment type

- Full-time preferred, but also accept contract, freelance, and part-time roles
  IF the position is fully remote.
- Skip contract, freelance, or part-time roles that are on-site or hybrid
  (non-remote), unless the job is in Israel (see Israeli jobs section).

## Work arrangement

- Accept if: fully remote (worldwide), OR relocation with visa
  sponsorship/support.
- Accept without explicit sponsorship mention if: the job is in Germany or
  another EU country AND the posting does not say "locals only", "no
  sponsorship", "must be authorized to work", or similar blockers. EU employers
  commonly sponsor without advertising it.
- Do NOT require both remote and relocation — either one is sufficient.
- SKIP if the role is remote but restricted to US and/or Canada
  residents/citizens only. Signals: "US only", "Canada only", "US/Canada only",
  "must reside in the US", "must be authorized to work in the US", "no visa
  sponsorship", "W2 only". If the posting is from a US or Canadian company but
  has no such restriction, treat it as acceptable.

## Seniority

- Senior roles preferred. If seniority level is not mentioned, assume it is a
  senior-level role and treat it as a match.
- Skip if explicitly junior or entry-level.

## Technology stack

- Must be iOS/macOS focused (Swift, SwiftUI, UIKit, Objective-C, Objective-C++,
  AppKit).
- Skip if the MAIN technology is a cross-platform framework (React Native,
  Flutter, Xamarin, Ionic, Kotlin Multiplatform). Mention of cross-platform as
  secondary/optional is fine.

## Industry

- Skip roles at crypto or Web3 companies.
- No other industry restrictions.

## Salary

- No salary filter — notify regardless of stated compensation.

## Israeli jobs

- Igor is already based in Israel; no relocation or sponsorship is required for
  Israeli roles.
- Accept all employment types (full-time, part-time, contract, freelance) for
  Israeli roles.
- Accept fully remote Israeli roles.
- Accept hybrid or on-site Israeli roles UNLESS the posting explicitly requires
  4 or more days per week in the office (e.g. "4 days/week in office", "5 days
  onsite", "fully on-site", "100% in-person"). If the posting is silent on
  office attendance, says only "hybrid" without specifying days, or requires 3
  or fewer days in office, treat it as acceptable.
- Crypto/Web3 exclusion and all other filters still apply.

## Timezone

- Igor is based in Israel (UTC+3). If the job requires strict US-only working
  hours, do NOT skip — include a note flagging the timezone mismatch so he can
  decide.
